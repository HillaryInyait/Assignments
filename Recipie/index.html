Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

For the pizza dough:
  1. 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  2. 1 1/2 cups warm water
  3. 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 1 teaspoon salt
  6. 1 teaspoon sugar
For the pizza sauce:
  1. 1 can crushed tomatoes
  2. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  3. 1 teaspoon dried basil
  4. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  5. Salt and black pepper, to taste
For the toppings:
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite vegetables (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  • Cooked and crumbled Italian sausage or pepperoni slices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Make the Pizza Dough:
    • In a small bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes.
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until the dough begins to come together.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place until it doubles in size.
  2. Prepare the Pizza Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, heat a little olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
    • Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasonings to taste.
  3. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go, usually around 245Â°C.
  4. Shape and Top the Pizza:
    • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two portions for two pizzas. Roll out each portion on a floured surface into your desired pizza shape and thickness.
    • Place the rolled-out dough on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet or pizza stone.
    • Spread a thin layer of the prepared pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
    • Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    • Add your favorite toppings such as vegetables and meat.
  5. Bake the Pizza:
    • Carefully transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper if using a baking sheet) onto the preheated oven or pizza stone. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two. Slice it up and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
